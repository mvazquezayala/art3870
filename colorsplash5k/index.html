You are about to get a tutorial on how to host a Color Run!

Find a great venue and organization to help fundraise money for. For example, TKE's philanthropy is St. Jude Children's Research Hospital!

Start making connections to build with other organizations on campus. We got the amazing support from EOP and other orgs on campus!

Try to reach out to nearby businesses for donations and sponsorships. TKE was able to recieve close to $3,000 in donations for the event!

Buy Color Powder, Runner Bibs, T-Shirts, Water, and have music to entertain your participants!

Create an Eventbrite page to sell your tickets online and be able to monitor guests count to make an accurate purchase of materials!

Set up the night before to avoid having to set up the day of, saves you time and stress! Also try to clean up after the event!

Have a group of supporting volunteers to help the event run smoothly!

Enjoy the Run and fundraise as much as you can. We fundraised $5,044!
